Why This Guitar is Special

In 2019, Fender launched the American Acoustasonic Telecaster, which became one of the most successful new product launches in the company’s history. Later that year, they released a few exotic wood versions, including this Cocobolo model, which sold out incredibly fast. The Limited Edition Exotic Acoustasonic Telecasters were a massive hit and have been in high demand ever since. This particular model, with its breathtaking Cocobolo top, is a true gem – a home run on top of a home run for Fender.

The Acoustasonic Telecaster represents the next era in Fender craftsmanship and innovation. Designed for fearless artists, it blends future technology with organic feel and playability, offering limitless new sounds and the classic Fender feel. Whether you’re a collector or a performer, this guitar is a rare opportunity to own a piece of Fender history.
